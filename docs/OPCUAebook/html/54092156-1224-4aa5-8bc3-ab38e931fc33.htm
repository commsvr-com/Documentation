What is the OPC e-book Sponsorship Program ?

OPC Unified Architecture e-book Sponsorship Program is an official, closest and most direct method for corporations or individuals to support the continues development of the OPC Unified Architecture e-book. The intention of the author and the publisher is to make OPC Unified Architecture more understandable.

In return the OPC Unified Architecture e-book will make an official acknowledgement and thanks for the donation via:

  • Sponsor’s logo (as appropriate according to sponsorship level),

  • Sponsor’s logo and website link on Thanks page on the OPC Unified Architecture e-book (as appropriate according to sponsorship level),

  • subtopic with links devoted to Sponsor on the OPC Unified Architecture e-book (as appropriate according to sponsorship level),

  • an official OPC Unified Architecture e-book logo button to place on the Sponsor's site:

    • For Gold Sponsor:

    • For Silver Sponsor:

  • information about the e-book and the Sponsor’s logo during vendor-independent OPC events organized by the Sponsee.

There are three sponsorship levels, defined by donation and in-return benefits of sponsorship.

Donations made to the Sponsorship Program must be coordinated with the OPC e-book Fundraising team (contact via email )

Sponsorship levels

  • Gold Sponsor

    • Your logo (max size: 300x150) on our OPC Unified Architecture e-book Thanks page and link in Gold Sponsors section

    • Your logo (max size: 100x50) in every OPC e-book page header

    • Subtopic containing Sponsor information with links and logo (max size: 300x150)

    • Sponsors name and logo in printed materials prepared for vendor-independent OPC events organized by the Sponsee

    • An official OPC Unified Architecture e-book Top Sponsor logo button to place on the Sponsor's site

    • Privilege of adding materials to the e-book (including author’s personal and affiliation information), reviewing materials and consulting the e-book content.

    • Privilege of using OPC UA e-book topic references in Sponsor software - every Gold Sponsor will get the list of the OPC UA e-book topics with unique identifiers, which might be used to make references in the software GUI and documentation.

  • Silver Sponsor

    • Your logo (max size: 150x75) on our OPC Unified Architecture e-book Thanks page and link in Silver Sponsors section

    • Subtopic containing Sponsor information with links and logo (max size: 150x75)

    • Sponsors name (without logo) in printed materials prepared for vendor-independent OPC events organized by the Sponsee

    • An official OPC Unified Architecture e-book Sponsor logo button to place on the Sponsor's site

  • Bronze Sponsor

    • Link to the Sponsor’s website on our OPC Unified Architecture e-book Thanks page in Bronze Sponsors section

Why should I sponsor the e-book?

The OPC Unified Architecture e-book Fundraising team provides the infrastructure for the OPC Unified Architecture e-book project - the technical support, hosting, code repositories, bug tracking systems, etc. While all of the administrative effort is currently through volunteers, we need financial assistance to purchase bandwidth and keep our servers running.

By sponsoring the OPC Unified Architecture e-book you show your support for our mission, which is the promotion, popularization and development of OPC Unified Architecture technology.

We want also to facilitate developers, integrators and end-users the access to clear and understandable information about OPC Unified Architecture.

Where will my sponsorship funds go ?

The funds will go for:

  • Content development

  • E-book technical support and maintenance

  • Servers and hardware

  • Bandwidth costs

  • Normal office expenses

  • Marketing and PR

How to become a sponsor?

If you are interested in supporting the OPC Unified Architecture e-book, please contact us via e-mail to get more information about the Sponsorship Program. To become a sponsor coordinate the amount of the donation and the sponsorship level with the Public Relations and Fundraising team. In the next step you will be asked to fill the application form and send us necessary information (depending on the sponsorship type). All information will be kept in strictest confidence.
